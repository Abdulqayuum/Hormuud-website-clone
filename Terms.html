TWO DECADES OF DRIVING CONNECTIVITY

Established in 2002, built by Somalis for Somalis, from humble beginnings Hormuud Telecoms has grown to be one a leading purpose-driven company in the Horn of Africa.

We are proud of our history. Emerging from a civil war, with just 250 shareholders and ten customer service centres, our vision to transform the lives of our communities has driven us to connect Somalis worldwide through a variety of products and services.

We were the first to introduce mobile money into Somalia, the first Somali telecommunications company to be licensed by the Somali Central Bank and the first to gain the GSMA’s Mobile Money Certification.

Looking forward

We’re committed to changing lives through connectivity and unlocking transformation for Somalia. And we’ve got exciting plans to achieve this mission in the next few years.

  1. Nationwide 4G rollout by 2023
  2. New fibre optic services
  3. Regional expansion
