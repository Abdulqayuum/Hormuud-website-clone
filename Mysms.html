Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose SMS over email marketing?
More Somalis have access to a phone than a computer or smartphone, so SMS is perfect for engaging a nationwide audience. Receiving a message straight to your phone is more personal and can cut through the noise of a cluttered email inbox. With our SMS platform, you can target your customers precisely according to their demographics and save SMS messages so they can be referred to at any time.
What are SMS campaigns used for?
Most of our customers use SMS campaigns for: meeting reminders, exam notifications, event announcements, business updates, personalized festival wishes, and special celebrations. The choice is yours! SMS is a great way to keep your customers updated with your latest business announcements.
How much does My SMS cost?
The cost per SMS is two cents ($0.02), which covers one text message of 160 characters.
